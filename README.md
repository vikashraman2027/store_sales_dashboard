# Superstore Sales Analysis Report

## Project Overview
The **Superstore Sales Analysis Report** is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, regional sales distribution, and product category insights using Superstore sales data.

This dashboard helps businesses understand key performance metrics and identify growth opportunities through data-driven insights and visualization.

---

# Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing regions and product categories
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Track sales trends over time
- Visualize geographical sales distribution

---

# Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence Reporting

## Visualizations Included

### 1. Sales by Segment
Pie chart visualization showing contribution from:
- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

### 2. Sales Trend by Order Date
Time-series analysis to track sales fluctuations over time.

### 3. Sales by Sub-Category
Bar chart showing sales performance across product sub-categories such as:
- Phones
- Chairs
- Storage
- Tables
- Accessories
- Appliances

### 4. Sales by State
Map visualization displaying geographical sales distribution across the United States.

### 5. Sales by Region
Donut chart analyzing sales contribution from:
- West
- East
- Central
- South

---

# Key Business Insights
- Consumer segment contributes the highest sales.
- West region generates the highest revenue.
- Phones and Chairs are top-performing sub-categories.
- Certain regions show lower profitability despite high sales volume.
- Sales show periodic spikes indicating seasonal demand trends.

---

# Business Recommendations
- Increase focus on high-performing product categories.
- Improve marketing strategies in low-performing regions.
- Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Enhance inventory planning based on sales trends.
